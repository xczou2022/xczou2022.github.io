Hello! My name is Xingchen Zou (邹星辰). I grew up in Sichuan (Szechuan), China, so you can infer that I love pandas! 
I discovered my innate interest in research when I learnt about the technology behind a smart phone in senir high school. I have strong practical hands-on skills and learning abilities, and I am more interested in applying theoretical techniques in practice, which is why I chose to study engineering.After working with Dr. Lu Haifeng (my thesis supervisor), Dr. Sima Jun( my headteacher) and Dr. Chi Yin, I realised that I was suited to do research and developed a strong interest in it and decided to pursue a PhD degree.

I am currently a Msc. student in Department of Civil Engineering, the University of Hong Kong, major in Structural Engineering. Currently I like to think about the connection between machine learning (as well as deep learning) and pratical engineering, and I think the computer vision and Cyber-Physical Internet (CPS) plays an important role in that. ​I am also interested in more areas.
